About Us
PT Daya Muda Agung (DMA), is one of the subsidiaries of the Mahakam Group that runs pharmaceutical and consumer products distributions with its 16 branches. 10 in Java, 3 in Sumatra, 1 in Sulawesi, 1 in Kalimantan, 1 in Bali and supported by 3 sub-distributors which distribute betadine product.

Our History
PT Daya Muda Agung, has distributed Betadine products since 1977. Along with the development of the Mahakam Beta Farma as a pharmaceutical factory. Besides Mahakam Beta Farma's products, DMA is also supported by Inkenas Agung, Beta Gasindo Agung, and other National Principals for Pharma and Consumer products.
